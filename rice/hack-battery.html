Measuring Cell Phone Power Consumption By Hijacking the Battery Interface

Felix Xiaozhu Lin <xzl at rice DOT edu>

The power consumption is arguably the most important issue for mobile phones nowadays. Developers and researchers usually need to know accurate, real-time system-level power consumption of cell phones. However, most of them don't have the access to proprietary battery emulators to do so. This article describes our measurement approach, that is, dissecting the battery and hijacking the battery interface. As a study case, I show how to measure N900's power by applying such approach to Nokia's BL-5J battery.
